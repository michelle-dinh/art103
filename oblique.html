Oblique Strategies & Process


Part 01 - Oblique Strategies | Part 02


Team Members


Ashley Rose, Jacqueline Regalado & Juan Guzman


During the first phase, our team was given three strategies to work with. We then had to create art responses that responded to our selected strategy.


Three Oblique Strategies


  • Discipline self-indulgence
    • |
  • Use an unacceptable color
    • |
  • Do we need holes?
    •

Our team had a difficult time choosing which strategy to go with. We originally picked the second option, however, we found that our ideas were very limiting in terms of execution. We thought that wearing nonconventional colors in various environments (e.g. wearing colorful clothing at a funeral) would be great idea, but only having photoshopped images as a result did not meet the objective.


We decided to go with the third option, "Do we need holes?", because it was very open-ended and we were able to come with even more ways to represent this.


OUTCOME - My Part


I created a series of mini comics that give the readers different scenarios that will help them decide whether or not holes are needed. Mini comics are a way to engage the audience by guiding them into a short storyline that may result in a devastating or satisfying experience.
